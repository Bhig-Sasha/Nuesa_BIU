Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about NUESA BIU

How do I join NUESA BIU?

All engineering students at BIU are automatically members. Visit our About page for more information on getting involved.

Where are your meetings held?

Meetings are held in the Engineering Lecture Theatre (ELT) every Thursday at 3 PM during the academic session.

How can I become an executive member?

Elections are held annually. Watch for announcements on our website and notice boards for nomination information.

Do you offer tutoring services?

Yes! We organize peer tutoring sessions. Check the Resources page for schedules and available tutors.
