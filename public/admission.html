Admission Guide

Begin Your Journey

Discover the requirements, rules, and timeline for joining the Faculty of Engineering at Benson Idahosa University.

Admission Requirements

Ensure you meet all the necessary requirements before applying to the Faculty of Engineering

1 UTME Requirements

  • Minimum JAMB score of 180
  • English Language
  • Mathematics
  • Physics
  • Chemistry

2 O'Level Requirements

  • 5 Credits in WAEC/NECO/NABTEB
  • Must include English Language
  • Must include Mathematics
  • Must include Physics & Chemistry
  • Obtained in not more than 2 sittings

3 Direct Entry

  • HND/ND with minimum of Upper Credit
  • JUPEB with minimum acceptable points
  • A'Level passes in Maths and Physics
  • Must meet all O'Level requirements

Faculty Rules & Regulations

Standard code of conduct for all engineering students

1
Dress Code
All engineering students must adhere to the corporate dress code. Workshop coats and safety boots are mandatory for all laboratory and workshop sessions.
2
Class Attendance
A minimum of 75% attendance is required in all registered courses to be eligible to write the semester examinations.
3
Examination Conduct
Students must present their valid ID cards during exams. Malpractice of any form will lead to immediate disciplinary action and possible expulsion.
4
Laboratory Safety
Strict adherence to safety protocols in all laboratories. No student is allowed to operate heavy machinery without supervision.
5
Academic Integrity
Plagiarism in assignments, projects, or reports is strictly prohibited and carries severe academic penalties.

Admission Timeline

Key dates and processes for the academic session

January - March

JAMB Registration

Register for UTME and select Benson Idahosa University as your first choice.

August - September

Post-UTME Screening

Apply and participate in the BIU internal screening exercise.

October - November

Admission List Release

Check your admission status on the JAMB CAPS and BIU portal.

November

Acceptance Fee Payment

Pay the non-refundable acceptance fee to secure your admission.

December

Course Registration

Complete your physical clearance and register your courses online.

January

Orientation & Resumption

Attend the mandatory orientation program for fresh engineering students.
